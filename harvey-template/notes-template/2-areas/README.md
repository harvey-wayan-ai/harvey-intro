Ongoing responsibilities and long-term commitments.

## What Goes Here?

**Definition:** Roles and responsibilities that require continuous attention without a finish line.

**Examples:**
- Work (Data Analyst at Zuma)
- AI & Automation Learning
- Personal Health & Fitness
- Finances
- Relationships

**Not Areas:**
- "Complete data certification" ‚Üí This is a Project (has end date)
- "Old meeting notes from 2023" ‚Üí This is Archive

---

## Area Files

Each area gets its own file for ongoing notes and tracking.

### Recommended Areas for Wayan:

1. **work-zuma.md** - Job responsibilities, ongoing tasks
2. **learning-ai.md** - Continuous learning in AI/automation/data
3. **personal-life.md** - Health, habits, relationships
4. **finances.md** - Budget, investments, income tracking
5. **creative-ideas.md** - Side projects, creative pursuits
